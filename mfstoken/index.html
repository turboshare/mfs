Here you can view recommended gas prices. Buying coins will increase both the Buy Price and Sell Price with 0.00000001 MATIC per bought coin once the transaction is complete.

3. Under "MFS Token Balance" you can see how many MFS you currently own. Note that when cashing out coins, the Buy Price and Sell Price drop afterwards, decreasing the value of your (and everyone else's) coins by 0.00000001 MATIC per coin.

4 .Every time MFS are bought or sold, a percentage of the fee from the bought/sold coins will be divided under the current MFS holders (Dividends).

5 .You can move your tokens into Dividends which then is stored as MATIC To do this click on the "Sell Tokens" button - and your tokens will be transferred into MATIC based upon the current sell price.

6 .You can also use your dividends to immediately purchase more MFS tokens by selecting "Buy with Dividends".

7 .To cash out your MFS, click the "Withdraw" button and follow the prompts.
